Public Index

Loosely maintained by Dylan Maher

This page is a simple public landing page for Terramax helper scripts, bootstrap wrappers, and app repositories. It is intentionally lightweight: direct links, short descriptions, and no extra framework overhead. Most of what is here is personal project work, experiments, and things in progress, so do not expect every project to be polished or finished.
